RISHIKESH

YOGA CAPITAL OF THE WORLD

Rishikesh is a small town located in Uttarakhand. It is home to several pilgrimage sites for the Hindus. Apart from being a holy city, Rishikesh is also known as the Yoga capital of the world.
Since it is situated along the banks of the holy river Ganga, Rishikesh is also a spiritual place where you can find inner peace and solace.
The mesmerizing sight of aartis and chanting on the ghat will certainly uplift your spirits preparing you for a long and adventurous day ahead in the mystical town of Rishikesh.


Places To Visit In Rishikesh


Lakshman Jhula

An iconic landmark in Rishikesh, this is where
Lakshman is believed to have crossed the River
Ganges on a jute rope.

  • Any month, early morning is the perfect time to be here for
    best views.

Tera Manzil Temple

Also known as Trimbakeshwar Temple, this spiritual abode
is home to many famous Hindudeities and is
located on the banks of the Ganges.

  • Your destination temple can go for any season but May is better.

The Beatles Ashram

Fondly known as Chaurasi Kutia,
the Beatles came here to practice transcendental meditation
under Maharishi Mahesh Yogi, and gifted
the world some of their best compositions.

  • March-April is the best time to visit this ashram.

Best Time to Visit

  • The best time to visit is between March and April and September to October.
  • Rishikesh is best avoided during the monsoon months from July to August, as it receives heavy rainfall. Rafting is also closed during this time.
  • Winter, from November until February, is cold but generally pleasant. So, bring woolens.
  • Those interested in yoga shouldn't miss the International Yoga Festival, held in Rishikesh in March each year.
  • Don't forget to attend Parmarth Niketan ashram (in the Swag Ashram area) to experience the Ganga Aarti (worship ritual with fire) every evening.
